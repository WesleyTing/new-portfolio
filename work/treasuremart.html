TreasureMart

Throughout my time at Treasure Mart, I have worked with Django, Python, HTML/CSS/JS and Boostrap, and themes such as Metronic, Sandbox, and Martfury. Below I have added a general overview of what I've done and new functions I have implemented.

Upsell Systems

Product Grid

To trigger the upsell feature, specific products must meet a certain level of matching defined across 5 levels, and only the selected items meeting this criteria will be displayed alongside the matching items.

Upsell at checkout

Depending on the product, different modal windows may display varying content or use different language, including disclaimers. This information is determined by the product's specifications (such as its type, count, and match), and is processed through a series of conditional statements to generate a clear and appropriate message for each modal.

Marketing Systems

Product banners with content changing on refresh.

Banner Customizations

With a simple simple but flexible back-end system utilizing Django admin that allows the entire banner to be changeable, from the image and the text all the way to background and positioning.

And custom buttons as well that suits the category/product's criteria.
